Our Origin Story

As a multifaceted company, Property's is both an NFT, as well as a Web3 development agency.

Our origins are deeply entrenched and remain in the NFT space, successfully releaing and selling out a collection of 6,000 NFTs in the form of virtual real estate. This has helped us cultivate a wonderful community, who we are passionate about creating immersive metaverse experiences for.

On the other side of the coin, as a Web3 development agency, Property's is also focused on onboarding brands and businesses into the emerging world of Web3. Using experience, practical knowledge, and technical skills, we help businesses harness the metaverse's potential through butting-edge consulting, implementing, and testing.

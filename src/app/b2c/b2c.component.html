    The Sandbox: Property's Social Hub

    A Dynamic New Way to Socialize

    The Social Hub is one of the main digital realms in our ecosystem. This is an entertainment space where creators, gamers, social butterflies, and even curious spectators can cultivate new connections, experiences, and opportunities, or simply just hang out.

    The Social Hub is also home to over 25 clubhouses, from the Kaiju Kingz clubhouse, to the Lazy Lions clubhouse. These clubhouses give each of our partner Web3 communities their own unique hangout, while also giving them the liberty to visit other clubhouses.

    Built in conjunction with LandVault, the Social Hub is the perfect place to “meet your neighbors'' and network with interesting communities, including your own. It also offers an array of entertainment, from arcades, to art exhibitions, and so much more.

    Our Explorations

    Thriving for expansion & growth

    In order to fulfill our vision of building a multi-metaverse, we’re constantly exploring platforms to diversify into. One of our latest ventures is 3D metaverse OnCyber, a platform that allows architects to build their own 3D world and experiences.

    We’re in the process of building curated, collectible pods on OnCyber for community members to be able to own, joining the likes of architects such as RTFTK and Arqui9. We're also building a shared community space on OnCyber. Here, we’ll showcase and promote work from talented artists, creators and builders in and outside our community, as well as display Property’s’ very own special collectibles, which will be available to purchase or bid on via OpenSea.

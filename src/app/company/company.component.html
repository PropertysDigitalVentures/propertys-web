Our Philosophy

Building on the next version of entertainment in the digital world

Property’s was formed to provide space in the metaverse for curious explorers of an upcoming digital paradigm shift.

We believe that interaction with humans via web3 infrastructure will change how the world will express itself and form new relationships online.

As a metaverse experience developer company & a 3.0 agency, we’re creating digital property, adventures & stories on top of different blockchain-based metaverse platforms, helping to discover the usability of metaverse networks, and onboard new web 3.0 interactives.
